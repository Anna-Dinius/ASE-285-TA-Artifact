# Anna Dinius's TA Artifact: Important Information

This document contains a brief explanation of the contribution I chose to submit for my TA artifact.

## Artifact

## Reasoning

Dr. Cho recently reworked all the course materials for ASE 285: Software Engineering and Security Fundamentals. However, a couple of the new documents he created to teach students about Software Engineering are incredibly long. There are three documents in particular, one that is 145 pages long, one that is 180 pages long, and one that is 350 pages long. Because of the length, I do not believe the students actually read through any of them. However, those documents contain important information they need to know for the class. So, as my TA artifact, I reworked the 145-page document and condensed the lessons down into a 10-page document.

## Process

Because I used AI to assist me with creating this artifact, I want to share my process so I can be completely transparent about how and why I used it.

Dr. Cho is a huge proponent of AI. He encourages all of his students to take advantage of it in his classes and frequently uses it himself (of course, he encourages **proper** use). Before beginning work on this artifact, I met with Dr. Cho, and he gave approval for my proposal of not only the artifact but also the use of AI to assist in its creation.
